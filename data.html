Key Takeaways






After careful analysis of the dataset, there are a number of conclusions that can be drawn

  • Found meteorites are much more common than fallen meteorites
  • The greatest concentration of meteorite finds occurs in the most populated areas of the earth
  • For this particular dataset, the most impactful variable is the human factor- most meteors have been found in more populated areas in recent history


An analysis that removes the human element from the data and focuses on the geologic history of the meteorites is possible but would require a much more robust scientific analysis of each meteorite find and is not able to be done with this particular dataset.
